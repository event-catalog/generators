The Cart Service is responsible for managing the cart for the Order Management Team.

<NodeGraph />

## Business Context

This service plays a critical role in our system by:
- Managing the cart for the Order Management Team
- Ensuring data consistency and integrity for cart operations
- Supporting compliance requirements for cart data management (GDPR, CCPA)
- Enabling personalization features across the platform

## Key Responsibilities

- Cart Management
  - Adding and removing items
  - Updating quantities
  - Calculating totals

## Technical Details

- **Storage**: PostgreSQL for cart data
- **Authentication**: Integrates with AuthenticationService for credential management
- **Compliance**: Implements data retention policies and GDPR requirements
- **Scalability**: Horizontally scalable with stateless design
- **Monitoring**: Prometheus metrics for cart operations and service health

## Dependencies

- **AuthenticationService**: For user credential management
- **NotificationService**: For user-related notifications
- **AuditService**: For tracking user data changes
