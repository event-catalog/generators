## Overview

The Users Team is our dedicated customer experience unit, focusing on making every user interaction with our products delightful and memorable. This team works closely with customers to understand their needs, gather feedback, and ensure their success with our platform. Through proactive support, educational content, and community engagement, we strive to create lasting relationships with our users.

## Responsibilities

### Key Responsibilities
- **Customer Support**: Provide exceptional first-line support through various channels (chat, email, phone).
- **User Education**: Create and maintain documentation, tutorials, and training materials.
- **Community Management**: Foster and grow our user community through events and forums.
- **Feedback Collection**: Gather and analyze user feedback to drive product improvements.
- **Success Metrics**: Track and improve key customer satisfaction metrics and user engagement.
- **Onboarding**: Design and optimize user onboarding experiences for new customers.
