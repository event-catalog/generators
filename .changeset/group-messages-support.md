Add `groupMessagesBy` option to group messages in the EventCatalog visualiser. OpenAPI supports `x-extension` (via `x-eventcatalog-group`) and `path-prefix` (derived from URL path). AsyncAPI supports `x-extension` (via `x-eventcatalog-group` on messages).
